A Thoughtful Approach

At Meat World RW, we aim to reconnect people with nature and their food. We've become too disconnected from the sources of our sustenance. Our farmers' practices directly impact the quality of our meat and the environment. It's not just about enjoying good food, but also knowing its origins and the impact of our choices on people and the planet. We founded Meat World RW to drive this change, knowing the industry needed a shift and believing we could 'be the change'.
